The learning experience in HKUST is very extraordinary. I learned a lot useful knowledge here and what more important is that I have dug out my real interest. I find I can easily lose myself in designing a program. For example, after learning some python language in the COMP 1021 course, I was fully absorbed by the assignment and I decided to develop it further. I added many extra features into my work for the assignment and during that process, I encountered quite a lot of difficulties. Although at that time I knew very little about programming, I still managed to work them out with some creative methods. The University also provided me a large number of opportunities to put my knowledge into practice. Take the ELEC 1100 as an example, it was the first time that I finished a whole project with my teammate. We designed and connected the logic circuit based on all the lectures we had taken. When our smart car really moved along the line as we expected finally, we really had a great sense of achievement. These experiences also improved my team spirit and enhanced my teamwork ability. Thanks to the effort I have paid on my on-campus learning, I earned me a very solid foundation and equipped myself with the ability to learn more things by myself.
